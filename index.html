Portfolio Projects

Project 1: Enterprise Curriculum Architecture & Needs Analysis

Curriculum Architecture Training Needs Analysis (TNA) Blended Learning

The Challenge: A premium global enterprise client needed to drastically reduce their new-hire onboarding time. The existing training caused cognitive overload and left new employees unprepared for live customer interactions and critical safety protocols.

The Solution & TNA: I conducted a comprehensive Training Needs Analysis (TNA) to balance the client's requested timeline with the operational risks of deploying unprepared staff.

  • Evaluation Redesign: I shifted the training focus from "memorizing facts" to "operational readiness," instituting a Pass/Fail gate for critical safety protocols and financial compliance.
  • Blended Learning Architecture: I designed a comprehensive 6-day "Proficiency Track" curriculum. This seamlessly integrated self-paced CBTs with interactive, instructor-led system simulations.

The Impact: By filtering content into "Must Memorize" versus "Must Reference," the new curriculum successfully certified new hires faster while significantly reducing costly operational errors.

Project 2: Interactive Storyboarding – "The Guest & The Journey"

Articulate 360 Scenario-Based Learning Storyboard Development

The Project: I developed an interactive storyboard for an e-learning module titled "The Guest & The Journey". The goal was to train customer-facing associates on the psychological and logistical timeline of a client's experience.

Design Highlights:

  • Cultural Decoding Simulator: Designed branching scenarios to teach associates how to adapt the "L.A.S.T." (Listen, Apologize, Solve, Thank) service recovery model to different cultural personas.
  • Persona Matching: Engineered an interactive activity where trainees listen to audio cues of complaints and match them to distinct personas to anticipate needs.
  • Timeline Mapping: Created a drag-and-drop chronological map of the customer's travel steps to foster cognitive empathy regarding travel fatigue.

Project 3: "TP Air" – Immersive Aviation Simulation

Unreal Engine (UE) Gamification 3D Interactive Design

The Project: To push the boundaries of adult learning, I utilize immersive 3D technologies to replicate high-stakes operational environments. I developed "TP Air," an interactive aviation simulation designed to train staff on spatial awareness and safety protocols.

Design Highlights:

  • Interactive Environment: Learners navigate a fully rendered aircraft cabin using standard WASD controls, interacting directly with environmental elements.
  • Task-Based Gamification: Users are assigned critical operational missions, such as identifying and closing open overhead compartments, securing window blinds, and locating designated seating.
  • Safety Protocol Reinforcement: By allowing learners to physically practice these procedures in a risk-free virtual environment, the module significantly boosts long-term retention and procedural muscle memory compared to traditional video training.
